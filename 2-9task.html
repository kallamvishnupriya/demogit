Mr Bean


Mr. Bean is a funny British TV character created and played by Rowan Atkinson. He is known for his silly actions, rarely speaks, and makes people laugh worldwide. Mr. Bean also stars in cartoons and movies, driving his iconic green Mini car.



Scinchan

Shinchan is a popular Japanese cartoon character from the anime Crayon Shin-chan. He is a naughty and funny five-year-old boy known for his silly jokes and mischief. The show is loved by kids worldwide for its humor and lighthearted stories.



Tom and Jerry


Tom is a cat who always tries to catch Jerry, a clever little mouse, but rarely succeeds. Their endless chase is filled with hilarious tricks and action. This classic cartoon has been loved by kids and adults for decades.



Oggy and Cockroches


Oggy is a lazy blue cat who just wants to relax, but three mischievous cockroaches constantly annoy him. The show is full of funny chases, pranks, and slapstick comedy. Its a favorite cartoon worldwide.
